# Wine For You and Wine For Me

## Purpose 
[This Shiny app](https://jdubchak.shinyapps.io/wine_rating_shinyapp/) was created for DSCI 532 (Visualization 2) to fulfill its usage scenario below.   

## Usage Scenario
Abby is hosting a dinner party to celebrate her parents' 30th wedding anniversary. She plans to have 10 of her parents' closest friends to the party, and four of them are wine connoisseurs. Before going to the liquor store, she wants to get a good idea of the best wines available within her budget. This app is designed for Abby. It allows her to navigate by price, score, country and/or grape type to find the best wines that will satisfy her guests.
